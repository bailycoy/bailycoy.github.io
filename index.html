My Summer 2021 Vacation

By: Baily Coy


Welcome to my website!


This page is dedicated to sharing the trips I was lucky enough to take this summer.

I took two trips back to back at the end of July 2021.


The first place I went was to Lake Tahoe, CA with my family.

We stayed at a family friend's cabin and did tons of fun stuff while we were there.


We obviously spent lots of time at the beach!


We also went to visit the 1960 Winter Olympics Village.


We all live in different places, so we had a great time all getting toegther and hanging out for a week!




After spending the week with my family, I flew back home to Boise and spend two days at home before taking off for trip number two!


My second vacation was a belated birthday trip to Silverwood Theme Park in Athol, ID.


I love rollercoasters and I convinced my boyfriend to drive to Northern Idaho just so we could ride Silverwood's brand new coaster called Stunt Pilot.


We spent two days at the theme park and got to ride tons of fun rides.


We also took a took a boat ride across the Coeur d'Alene Lake one evening.


Thanks for looking back on these fun summer memories with me! I am lucky to have such amazing people in my life to have fun with!
